Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

"Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatics spices.
This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal,,and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp."

"The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors - sweet, sour, salty and spicy."
-Chef Somchai-

Ingredients:

  • Lemongrass:2 stalks
  • Thai basil:1 cup
  • Kaffir lime leaves:3 leaves
  • Shrimp:500g

Preparation:

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Addlemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Season with fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.
  5. Garnish with Thai basil and serve HOT.

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4 cups of fish sauce adds a authentic Thai flavor.
  • 1
    • tablespoon of chili paste that gives the soup its signiture heat.
